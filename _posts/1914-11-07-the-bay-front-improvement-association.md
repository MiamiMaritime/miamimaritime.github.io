---  
title: The Bay Front Improvement association  
tags:  
  - Nov 1914  
---  
  
The Bay Front Improvement association has plans for filling the shore out 300 to 500 feet into the bay from first street to Miramar. Dr. Jackson is holding up authorizing his 110 feet of frontage to be filled.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

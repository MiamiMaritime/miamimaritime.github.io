title: The Colonia with 1600 miles  
tags:  
  - Aug 1920  
---  
  
The Colonia, with 1600 miles of cable, rides at anchor 4 miles offshore with the American destroyers nearby. Permission to begin laying cable from 3 miles offshore through neutral waters is denied.

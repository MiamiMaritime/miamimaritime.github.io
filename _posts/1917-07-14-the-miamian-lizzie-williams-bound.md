title: The Miamian Lizzie Williams bound  
tags:  
  - Jul 1917  
---  
  
The Miamian Lizzie Williams, bound for Cuba with a cargo of lumber, is rammed and sunk by a tanker 15 miles northeast of Carey's North Light.

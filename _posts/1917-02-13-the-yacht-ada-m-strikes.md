title: The yacht Ada M strikes  
tags:  
  - Feb 1917  
---  
  
The yacht Ada M strikes the submerged wreckage of the Lagona [Lugano] punching a hole in her hull. With the help of the yacht Billeo, Ada M is patched and brought to Pelsang's boat yard on the river.

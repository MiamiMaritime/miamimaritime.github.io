---  
title: The Chamber of Commerce continues  
tags:  
  - Mar 1917  
---  
  
The Chamber of Commerce continues its push for 25 feet of water in the Miami harbor. Worried about the war; they request two guns to be stationed at the bar and a patrol boat on duty in Biscayne Bay.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

---  
title: The Deerings buy Pumpkin Key  
tags:  
  - May 1917  
---  
  
The Deerings buy Pumpkin Key, Fisher and Kotcher buy Cocolobus Key and Tatums buy Key Largo. All are forming fishing clubs. Tatums are building homes and a golf course on the extreme north end of Key Largo.

---  
title: The 3-masted schooner Alice R  
tags:  
  - Aug 1915  
---  
  
The 3-masted schooner Alice R. Phillips is spotted by the tug Peoria inside the outer reef with the flag flying union down. In a leaking condition she is towed to Key West.

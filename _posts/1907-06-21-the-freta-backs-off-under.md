---  
title: The Freta backs off under  
tags:  
  - Jun 1907  
---  
  
The Freta backs off under her own power from being hard aground 100 feet from the wrecked hull of the Haraldine [Haroldine]. The captain refuses assistance from the wreckers and wires the owners and underwriters.

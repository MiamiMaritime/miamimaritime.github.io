---  
title: Fourteen old Spanish cannons recovered  
tags:  
  - Sep 1919  
---  
  
Fourteen old Spanish cannons, recovered by Captain Webster in 30 feet of water a few miles north of Careysfort Light, will be used as mooring anchors for tugs, barges and buoys while working on Government Cut.

title: The J B McCrary Construction  
tags:  
  - Nov 1918  
---  
  
The J. B. McCrary Construction Company's dredge is building the base for the Cape Sable highway near the Monroe County line. Monroe County has issued bonds to continue the highway to between Middle and East Cape.

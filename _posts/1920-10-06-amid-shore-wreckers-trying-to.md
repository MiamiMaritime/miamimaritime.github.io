Amid shore wreckers trying to hack out valuables with axes, and passing steamers trying to claim Webster's schooner, the Robinson is towed to Caesar's Creek then to inside Elliott's Key for a temporary rudder. The schooner, valued at $50,000, was first located by a flying boat. Having been abandoned by her owners, she will be renamed by the buyer. [Details on wrecking techniques.]

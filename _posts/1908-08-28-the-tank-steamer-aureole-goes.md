---  
title: The tank steamer Aureole goes  
tags:  
  - Aug 1908  
---  
  
The tank steamer Aureole goes on a sand bar south of Fowey Rocks. She is floated at high tide after pumping her creosote cargo overboard. All wrecking assistance is refused.

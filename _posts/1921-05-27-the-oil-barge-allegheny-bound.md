---  
title: The oil barge Allegheny bound  
tags:  
  - May 1921  
---  
  
The oil barge Allegheny, bound for Miami with 3900 barrels of gasoline and kerosene, sinks 225 miles west of Key West. Her sistership, Mingo, foundered on Mobile Bar a year ago.

title: The 260282-foot 4-masted schooner Fantome  
tags:  
  - Oct 1998  
---  
  
The 260[282]-foot, 4-masted schooner Fantome has not been heard from since 27 October. The Fantome, owned by Miami based Windjammer Cruises, was trying to avoid a hurricane off Honduras.  
  
Newspapers: **Miami Morning News or The Miami Herald**  
  
Page: **20**, Section: **A**

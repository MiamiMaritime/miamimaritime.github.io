---  
title: A note in a bottle  
tags:  
  - May 1916  
---  
  
A note in a bottle found near Salt Key, Cuba, states: "...all hope is lost on the American schooner Thomas B. Garland, January 31, 1916." The ship has not been heard from since departing Tampa in 1915.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

title: The tug Salvor Captain Webster  
tags:  
  - Sep 1919  
---  
  
The tug Salvor, Captain Webster, may have located the derelict and abandoned mahogany laden schooner Hugh Depaynes in the Bahamas. He will tow her to Key West where Webster's wrecking outfit will lay claim.

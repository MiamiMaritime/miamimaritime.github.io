---  
title: The Canadian schooner Leonard Parker  
tags:  
  - Sep 1913  
---  
  
The Canadian schooner Leonard Parker, which was on the reef for several days at Ragged Key, burns and sinks. The ship carried little insurance and the crew is brought to Miami.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

---  
title: The schooner Fearless is wrecked  
tags:  
  - Dec 1915  
---  
  
The schooner Fearless is wrecked on the rocks at Gun Key cut, Bahamas. The schooner William H. Albury rescues all the passengers and crew before the ship sank.

---  
title: The schooner Francis E rams  
tags:  
  - May 1914  
---  
  
The schooner Francis E rams schooner Centennial breaking rigging and the bowsprit. The Francis E continues but runs on two sandbars on the way to Cape Florida. She returns to port before the day is over.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

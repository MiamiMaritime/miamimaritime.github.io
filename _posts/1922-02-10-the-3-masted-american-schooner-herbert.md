---  
title: The 3-masted American schooner Herbert  
tags:  
  - Feb 1922  
---  
  
The 3-masted American schooner Herbert May goes aground in 13 feet of water near Marquesas. Apparently hogged, only the lumber cargo in the hold might be salvaged.

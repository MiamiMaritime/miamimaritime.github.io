---  
title: The St Lucie sunk in  
tags:  
  - Oct 1906  
---  
  
The St. Lucie sunk in the bay near the mouth of Caesars Creek, 25 dead. Debris and foundering smacks are everywhere. Extension quarterboat number 4 collapsed and sank near Long Key, many of the 150 men on board are lost. Every vessel at Long Key is sunk or beached. Steamers Virginia and Biscayne are bringing in the survivors and dead.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

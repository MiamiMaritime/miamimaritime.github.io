Wreckers Take Notice: The U.S. marshall at Miami warns all parties interested in wrecking not to trespass upon the American schooner, Star of the Sea. Said vessel is aground on French Reef.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

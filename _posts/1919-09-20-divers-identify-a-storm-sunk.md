---  
title: Divers identify a storm sunk  
tags:  
  - Sep 1919  
---  
  
Divers identify a storm sunk steamer in 40 feet of water near Rebecca Shoals light as the Valbanera. There is no trace of the 300 passengers and 150 crewmen.

---  
title: The Valmaseda is appraised at  
tags:  
  - Aug 1910  
---  
  
The Valmaseda is appraised at $45000. The wreckers; Miami Yacht and Machine Works, Philip Lee, Sam Gerock, "Bubba" Smart and Charles Thompson, will split about $20,000. "This has been the finest specimen of wrecking work ever done...".

title: The tramp steamer Evelyn is  
tags:  
  - Sep 1910  
---  
  
The tramp steamer Evelyn is grounded on French Reef. Wreckers discharge cargo, put an anchor out and at high tide the 250-foot vessel pulls herself free. The cargo is reloaded and the vessel proceeds to Key West.

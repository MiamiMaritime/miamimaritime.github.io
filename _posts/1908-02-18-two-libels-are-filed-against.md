---  
title: Two libels are filed against  
tags:  
  - Feb 1908  
---  
  
Two libels are filed against Bayamo and cargo. Wreckers want salvage for coming to assist even though they were refused by the captain. The Bayamo is still aground and cargo continues to come into Miami.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

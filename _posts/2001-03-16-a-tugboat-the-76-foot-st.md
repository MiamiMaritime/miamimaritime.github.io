A tugboat [the 76-foot St. Marks guiding an empty 371-foot fuel barge on a return trip from Turkey Point, 21Mar01] breaks a sewer pipe at the mouth of the Miami River. Many Key Biscayne beaches are closed due to the spill.  
  
Newspapers: **Miami Morning News or The Miami Herald**

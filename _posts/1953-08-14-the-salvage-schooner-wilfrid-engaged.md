The salvage schooner Wilfrid, engaged in salvaging lead ingots from the torpedoed Ocean Venus, is aground in Cape Canaveral Inlet.  
  
Newspapers: **Miami Morning News or The Miami Herald**  
  
Page: **8**, Section: **A**

title: The Norwegian freight steamer Senstad  
tags:  
  - Jul 1911  
---  
  
The Norwegian freight steamer Senstad with a load of coal is aground on Tennessee Reef. 800 tons of coal is cast overboard to lighten her. 36 sails and 250 men are hovering about the site with offers of assistance.

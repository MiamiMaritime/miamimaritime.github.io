title: A water barge towed by  
tags:  
  - Dec 1905  
---  
  
A water barge towed by the Biscayne strikes a pile and sinks off Bear's Cut. Biscayne pushes her into shallow water before she sinks. The St. Lucie leaves with a crew to repair her.

The oil tanker F. E. Asche is aground on Mantanilla [Matanilla] Shoals, Bahamas. The USCG cutter Tallapoosa found the steamship Frederick Luckenbach adrift and listing 54 miles from Tampa. The crew of the schooner Cerrito reached Pinar del Rio after abandoning their vessel off the sands of San Luis.

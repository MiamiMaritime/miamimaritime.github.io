---  
title: The 450-foot steamer Indore goes  
tags:  
  - May 1912  
---  
  
The 450-foot steamer Indore goes aground and is floated by wreckers off French Reef. Reported to be the largest ship to run aground on the Florida coast.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

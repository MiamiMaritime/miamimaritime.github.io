---  
title: The auxiliary 3-masted schooner Jeannette  
tags:  
  - Mar 1919  
---  
  
The auxiliary 3-masted schooner Jeannette sinks with 400 tons of cement 15 miles southeast of Fowey Rock Light. The crew sails to Miami Beach in a lifeboat.

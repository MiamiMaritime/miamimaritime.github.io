F. Siems, U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey, advises to dig a cut in the upper bay as Government Cut is deepened to lessen the tidal current. Currently the USCG survey boat Hydrographer is sounding between 15 and 100 fathoms from Arch Creek to Fowey Rock.

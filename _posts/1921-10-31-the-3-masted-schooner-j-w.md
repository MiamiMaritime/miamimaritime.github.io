title: The 3-masted schooner J W  
tags:  
  - Oct 1921  
---  
  
The 3-masted schooner J. W. Somerville is found capsized and dismasted 40 miles off Dry Tortugas. USCG cutter Tallapoosa takes her in tow after searching but not finding any crew members.

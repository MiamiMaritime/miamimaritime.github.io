---  
title: The 180-foot steam yacht Columbia  
tags:  
  - Jan 1907  
---  
  
The 180-foot steam yacht Columbia arrives off the Cape. Captain Ball [Klondike] goes down to pilot the yacht to an anchorage in the lower bay. This is the largest yacht to enter the bay.

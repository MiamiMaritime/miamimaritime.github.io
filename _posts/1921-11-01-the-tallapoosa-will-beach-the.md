The Tallapoosa will beach the J. W. Somerville, found 43 miles southwest of Sands Key Light, at Kingfish Shoals. The disabled schooner Virginia, owned by the Pensacola Fish Company, was towed to Dry Tortugas. The dismasted schooner Osceola was towed to Key West by the steamer Algiers.

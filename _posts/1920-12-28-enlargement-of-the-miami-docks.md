Enlargement of the Miami docks is necessary as ships berth abreast. Schooners: Sarah E. Douglass, Harvester and P. J. McLaughlin; auxiliary yachts: Mystery J and Alcoyne; steamer City of Miami, cable ship Robert C. Clowry; destroyer Harding; tug Cayo Cristo.

---  
title: The Canadian schooner Melba went  
tags:  
  - Apr 1916  
---  
  
The Canadian schooner Melba went aground on Molasses Reef on 29 March. The wrecking master, W. Johnson, is jettisoning cargo but there is little hope of saving her and abandonment is probable.

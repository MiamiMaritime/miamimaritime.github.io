title: Crosland Steamship Company will establish  
tags:  
  - Jul 1912  
---  
  
Crosland Steamship Company will establish regular freight service if the city will furnish an 11-foot depth to the Crosland dock.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

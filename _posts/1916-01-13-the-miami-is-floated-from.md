title: The Miami is floated from  
tags:  
  - Jan 1916  
---  
  
The Miami is floated from the sand bar and is berthed at the P. & O. docks. Miami - Nassau schooners are busy while the Miami is landlocked in the bay.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**

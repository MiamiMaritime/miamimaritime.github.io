The steamer Miami starts her 27th season of service between Miami and the Bahamas. The 250-foot steamer, owned by Peninsular & Occidental Steamship Company, was built in 1895 by Henry Flagler.  
  
Newspapers: **Daily Miami Metropolis, The Miami Metropolis (daily), The Daily Metropolis or Miami Daily Metropolis**
